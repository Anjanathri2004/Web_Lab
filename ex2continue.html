Antenna TV is an American digital television network owned by Nexstar Media Group.
The network's programming consists of classic television series, primarily sitcoms, from the 1950s to the 1990s.
Antenna TV's programming and advertising operations are headquartered in the WGN-TV studios in Chicago.
The network's operations are overseen by Sean Compton, who serves as the president of networks for Nexstar.
